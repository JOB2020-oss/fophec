Our Services

  2. Our Services

Environmental Education

We implement programs of environmental education in primary and secondary schools, teacher training and sensitization of local communities

Patnership with community

We provide other initiatives or necessary services, whether independently or in conjunction with other organizations which support community self-help efforts and otherwise carry out the general purpose of the Organization to help society live in safe environment.

Promotion Of Eco-Tourism

We facilitate eco-tourist routes in areas worth of conservation and high in biodiversity and promote local income-generating activities such as beekeeping, animal husbandry, tourist facilities and handcraft.


Preservation Of Nature

We Preserve nature by implementing conservation initiatives such as deforestation and reaforestation by mobilizing the community to take action such as plantation of trees

Tree planting

We collaborate with schools to provide immediate supervision and engage pupils and students in planting and taking care of the planted and existing trees in their school premises and vicinity.
