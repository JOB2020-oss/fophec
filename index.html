Our Vision:

We envision a wealthy, healthy and well protected environment supporting a sustainable health society and economy.

Our Mission:

    Who we are ?

    The Foundation for Peoples Health and Environmental Conservation Tanzania

    The Foundation for Peoples Health and Environmental Conservation Tanzania(FOPHEC TANZANIA) is a Non Governmental Organization. With its operation in United Republic of Tanzania. The organization is visioned in the creation of a well proteceted environment while suporting a well sustainable society and economy.

    We are engaged in:

    • Dissemination of agro-forestry techniques as an alternative to forest resource collection./li>
    • Implementation of programs of environmental education in primary and secondary schools, teacher training and sensitization of local communities.
    • promotion of the organization as non-profit entity to be the voice speaking on behalf of environment activities
